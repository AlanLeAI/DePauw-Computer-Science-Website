Computer Science is for everyone

Computer science emphasizes problem-solving through software and hardware across different sectors. DePauw CS alumni find opportunities in various industries and higher education. Visualized Learning
This website offers visualizations of various data structures used in Computer Science, providing students with a more intuitive understanding of complex concepts.
Applied Learning
This website also offers coding practice problems transform theoretical concepts into practical skills, allowing students to actively engage with the subject matter and deepen their understanding.
